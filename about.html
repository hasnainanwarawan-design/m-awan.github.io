About Our School

Al-Noor Islamic Public School is dedicated to nurturing students with strong moral values, academic excellence, and a deep connection to Islamic teachings.

Founded on the principles of knowledge, compassion, and discipline, we aim to develop well-rounded individuals who contribute positively to society.

Our Mission
